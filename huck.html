    What do we mean by hack?

    To hack is also to illegally break into someone's computer. The Old English root word is haccian, which means “to cut into pieces, ” but hack also means to cough frequently. A tickle in your throat can make you hack. If you call someone a hack, you mean they're not great at what they do — especially writing.
